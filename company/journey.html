Our Journey.

From a Bengaluru garage to powering enterprise transformations across four continents — this is the story of how PromethByte came to operationalize data intelligence.

2023

The Genesis

Founded in Bengaluru by a team of ex-enterprise architects who saw a critical gap: organizations had access to more data than ever, but lacked the transformation engineering required to turn it into intelligence. PromethByte was born to close that gap — not with tools, but with methodology.

2024

Scaling to Enterprise

Launched our proprietary Transformation Suite — a battle-tested framework for phased legacy-to-cloud migrations with zero-downtime guarantees. Onboarded our first Fortune 500 client and expanded to 60+ engineers across three delivery centers. Achieved SOC 2 Type II compliance and AWS Advanced Partner status.

2025

The Intelligence Era

Integrated agentic AI and RAG pipeline capabilities into our transformation framework. Launched dedicated verticals for Financial Services, Healthcare, and SaaS. Crossed the 150-client milestone and expanded to serve enterprises across North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia. 4.2 petabytes processed daily.

The People Behind the Platform.

A 120+ person team of architects, engineers, and data scientists united by a single mission: making data transformation accessible, repeatable, and impactful.
